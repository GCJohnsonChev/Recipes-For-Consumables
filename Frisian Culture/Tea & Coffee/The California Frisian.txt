Mix at home

Take 3 ounces of ice
Take 6 ounces of Pomegranate
Take 6 ounces of Blue Berries
Take 18 ounces of Latte mixed with milk
Take 3 organic green tea bags

Blend everything together and let the mix soak in with 4 table spoons of organic cane sugar powder
and the 3 organic green tea bags for 5 minutes then add some ice and shake.
Mix in some liquid organic chocolate / mocha.
